Update 1: Teacher Shortage Report

According to recent reports, there is a need for an additional 689,000 teachers in primary schools to meet the RTE norms.

Update 2: Student Performance Concerns

ASER reports indicate that 78% of 3rd-grade students and 50% of 5th-grade students are unable to read the text of 2nd-grade level.

Update 3: High Dropout Rates

Approximately 1.4 million students drop out of schooling between the ages of 6-11 years, highlighting the need for improved retention strategies.

Update 4: Accountability Issues

There is a significant lack of accountability in the Sarva Shiksha Abhiyaan, which contributes to poor learning outcomes and low attendance rates.
